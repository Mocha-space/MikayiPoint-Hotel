About us

Welcome to Mikayi Point Restaurant, a hidden gem in Kisumu West, Holo. Escape city life and unwind in our serene, natural setting near the iconic Kit Mikayi. We offer the finest organic dishes, ensuring each meal is delicious and nourishing. Surrounded by lush greenery and birdsong, our guests enjoy a true retreat. Our venue is perfect for celebrations like weddings, baby showers, and birthday parties, with personalized service for every event.

Enjoy our delectable food paired with authentic local beers, wines, and spirits. Come experience the charm of Mikayi Point Restaurant.
